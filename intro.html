What is Basketball?

Basketball is a team sport in which two teams of five players each, compete to shoot a basketball through the defender's basket to score points, while preventing the opposing team from doing so. The team that has more points at the end of the game wins.

Invention of Basketball

In December 1891, Dr James Naismith was trying to keep his gym class active on a rainy day. He invented a new game in which players would pass a ball to teammates and try to score points by tossing the ball into a basket mounted on a wall. The game was named basketball as one of the players from the original first game, suggested to Naismith that the game be called "basketball", and Naismith agreed as the game was played with a basket and a ball.
