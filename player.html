The Greatest NBA Player of All Time

Michael Jordan

"Black Jesus"

Michael Jordan played fifteen seasons in the NBA, mostly with the Chicago Bulls, and a bit with the Washington Wizards at the end of his career. He was integral in popularizing the NBA around the world in the 1980s and 1990s, becoming a global cultural icon in the process. In his career, he got 2 three-peats and never lost a Finals series. He is regarded by most as the Greatest Of All Time (GOAT) of basketball.
